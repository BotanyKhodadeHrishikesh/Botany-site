TYBSc Botany

Programme Outcomes (PO — Science)

  • Design investigations and interpret literature critically.
  • Use statistical/analytical tools for data interpretation.
  • Communicate research through reports & presentations.

Programme Specific Outcomes (PSO — Botany)

  • Connect molecular mechanisms to plant development.
  • Evaluate evolution & ecology using real datasets.
  • Prepare publishable figures and references.

Course Outcomes (CO)

Paper I: Genetics & Molecular Biology
  • Solve Mendelian & non-Mendelian inheritance problems.
  • Describe DNA replication, transcription, translation.
  • Explain gene regulation and mutations.
Paper II: Developmental Biology, Evolution & Ecology
  • Outline developmental patterning in plants.
  • Discuss evolutionary mechanisms & speciation.
  • Analyze population/community ecology metrics.
